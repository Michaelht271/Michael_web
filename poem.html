The Digital Dawn

January 15, 2024 | Technology
In circuits deep and silicon dreams,
Where binary whispers flow in streams,
A world of ones and zeros bright,
Illuminates the digital night.

Through networks vast and servers high,
Data packets race across the sky,
Each pixel perfect, each byte complete,
As virtual and reality meet.

In this realm of endless code,
Where innovation's seeds are sowed,
We build tomorrow's dreams today,
As technology lights the way.
